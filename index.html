Our Story

The recent outbreak of coronavirus is a global tragedy first and foremost. We must all do what we can to minimise the spread and effects of the virus, and to support each other during these difficult times. But we must not ignore the lessons coronavirus has taught us.

At the time of writing, the number of global coronavirus deaths has just passed 60,000. Of course this figure is expected to rise, and is already a terrible tragedy. But coronavirus is not the only cause worth changing for.

For the billion people living in slums today, for the millions of children who die every year from preventable causes and for the hundreds of thousands of people who die each year as a result of climate change, we must act. Our goal cannot be to go back to ‘normal’.

The rupture in our daily lives caused by coronavirus is a chance - perhaps our last chance - to start shaping a better world and a better future.

To create real and sustainable change, we all need to work together. Disjointed efforts can only get us so far; we need co-ordinated action.

The App

This is where now-U comes in. By setting weekly challenges as part of monthly campaigns, we can all work together to tackle key issues relating to:

  1. People
  2. Animals and Nature
  3. The future of humans and our planet

now-U users will be invited to learn more about these issues, and to participate in weekly personal challenges, in addition to setting challenges for influencers, companies and governments.

By combining the small actions of each of us, the now-U community can play a huge part in driving positive change. We cannot waste this opportunity. Let’s come together, not just in the fight against coronavirus, but in the fight for the future of our planet and all its inhabitants.
